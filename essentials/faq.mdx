## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Here are some of the most common questions users have about FINN AI.

### General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="robot" title="What is FINN AI?">
    FINN AI is an AI-powered assistant that provides **real-time market insights** for stocks, crypto, options, and more. It is designed to help traders and investors analyze market trends but does not provide financial advice.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="globe" title="Do I need to install anything to use FINN AI?">
    No, FINN AI is a **web-based AI agent**. Simply visit the website, log in, and start using it—no downloads or installations required.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Market Insights & Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="chart-line" title="How does FINN AI generate insights?">
    FINN AI analyzes **real-time market data**, financial news, and historical trends using AI models. It provides insights based on market conditions but does not make predictions or guarantee outcomes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="database" title="Where does FINN AI get its market data from?">
    FINN AI pulls data from **trusted financial sources**, including stock market APIs, cryptocurrency exchanges, and macroeconomic indicators.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="brain" title="Can FINN AI execute trades for me?">
    No, FINN AI does **not execute trades** or manage portfolios. It provides market insights to help you make informed decisions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Financial & Legal

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="triangle-exclamation" title="Does FINN AI provide financial advice?">
    No, FINN AI provides **market insights but is not a financial advisor**. Users should always do their own research or consult with a licensed professional before making investment decisions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="landmark" title="Is FINN AI compliant with financial regulations?">
    Yes, FINN AI operates within compliance guidelines but is **not a registered financial institution or brokerage**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="user-shield" title="How is my data protected?">
    FINN AI follows **strict privacy policies** and does not store or sell sensitive financial data. For more details, visit our [Privacy Policy](/docs/legal/privacy).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Troubleshooting & Support

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="bug" title="I received an error message. What should I do?">
    If you encounter an issue, try refreshing the page or checking your internet connection. If the problem persists, contact **support** for assistance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="envelope" title="How can I contact support?">
    You can reach out through our [Support Page](/docs/support) for any technical or account-related inquiries.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
