## Need Help? Contact FINN AI Support

If you encounter any issues while using FINN AI or have questions about its features, we’re here to help.

### How to Get Support

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="envelope" title="Submit a Support Request">
    You can submit a support request through our [Support Portal](/docs/support). Please provide details about your issue, including screenshots if applicable.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="bug" title="Report a Bug">
    Found a technical issue? Report it through our [Bug Report Form](/docs/support/bug-report). Our team will investigate and work on a fix.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="users" title="Join the Community">
    Connect with other traders and investors using FINN AI in our [Community Forum](/docs/community). Share insights, ask questions, and discuss market trends.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out, check our [FAQ section](/docs/faq) to see if your question has already been answered.
