## Get Started with FINN AI

FINN AI is a web-based AI assistant designed to provide real-time market insights. No installation is required—simply access the platform and start analyzing stocks, crypto, and options.

### Accessing FINN AI

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="globe" title="Open FINN AI">
    FINN AI is accessible directly via the web. Visit our official platform and log in to start using the AI assistant immediately.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="user" title="Sign In or Create an Account">
    If required, sign in using your credentials or create a new account to access premium features.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Using FINN AI

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="comments" title="Ask Market Questions">
    Simply type your questions into the chat interface. You can ask about stock trends, crypto market sentiment, options flow, and more.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Get Real-Time Insights">
    FINN AI provides instant market analysis. Use the insights to enhance your investment research but always cross-check information before making financial decisions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="lightbulb" title="Be Specific">
    The more detailed your question, the more precise the AI’s response will be. Try to specify stocks, time frames, or market events.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="sync" title="Follow Up">
    Ask follow-up questions to refine your insights and gain a deeper understanding of market trends.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="bullhorn" title="Stay Updated">
    New features and enhancements may be introduced over time. Keep an eye on updates to get the most out of FINN AI.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
